Core Capabilities

What DustVault Does

Three integrated capabilities that handle everything from detection to re-engagement to compliance documentation — without touching your operations team.

Automated Dormancy Detection

We ingest your account ledger — no PII required — and cross-reference every account against all 50 state dormancy windows. At-risk accounts surface in 48 hours. No manual review. No spreadsheets.

Automated Member Re-Engagement

Before forfeiture triggers, we deploy personalized outreach — exact balance, eligible spend categories, specific deadline. Email first, SMS follow-up. Zero human involvement required on your end.

State Escheatment Filing Package

For accounts crossing the threshold, we prepare complete state filing documentation — exemptions, partial remittance calculations, due diligence notices — across all 50 states. Your team reviews and submits.
